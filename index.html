About Our Mission

The Global Cat Rescue Alliance was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to ensure every cat around the world has access to care, shelter, and love. What began as a small operation in London has grown into an international network of veterinarians, shelters, and volunteers dedicated to feline welfare.

We believe that by rescuing one cat at a time, we can create a world where no cat suffers from neglect, abuse, or abandonment. Our comprehensive approach includes emergency rescue, medical care, rehabilitation, adoption services, and community education.
